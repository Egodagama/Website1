We, the Geospatial People

Under the main theme of GEOWEEK 2021, a Round Table Discussion on “The Meeting Point: WE, GEOSPATIAL PEOPLE” has being organized in order to make a Platform for the academia, industrial experts and students etc. in order to share their knowledge and experiences about current trends, applications and new technologies in the field of Geo-spatial science and technology. In this, we hope to open up a forum for everyone engaged in this versatile industry under one umbrella with the intention to build up strong and inter-collaborations.

Getting to Know ArcGIS Online Tools and Apps

November 22, 24 & 25, 2021


Faculty of Geomatics




Join us for an amazing training opportunity..!

In collaboration with GIS Solutions (Pvt) Ltd., the Faculty of Geomatics is organizing its first virtual workshop for the Professional GIS user community in Sri Lanka, for the 1st annual activity, GEOWEEK2021.


Background:


ESRI being the global leader in GIS, location intelligence, and mapping technology, provides useful and very popular tools such as ArcGIS Online, Survey 123 and Dashboards to leverage the power of WebGIS technologies. The series of workshops cover the fundamentals and methods on these online tools showing how to perform geospatial activities wisely and disseminate final products to a wider audience smartly.

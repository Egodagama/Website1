GeoTalk

GeoTalk is one of the key events in GEOWEEK which is a platform to share experience and knowledge of scholars and professionals. This session contains five plenary speeches and is followed by a panel discussion at the end of the session. There are five (05) invited speakers who will deliver their talks for 25-30 minutes on concurrent topics in their specializations.

GeoTalk will be conducted hybrid (on-site and online) mode. The session will be streaming real-time through online digital platforms, you-tube and Facebook. The Media Studio will be ready for on-site participants and the session will be moderated by a Faculty member. Join with Zoom
